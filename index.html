Preserving Heritage Through Craft

Located in the beautiful Phatthalung province of Southern Thailand, Thale Noi Traditional Hand Craft has been preserving and promoting authentic Thai handicrafts for over three decades. Our artisans use time-honored techniques passed down through generations.

Each piece in our collection represents not just exceptional craftsmanship, but also the rich cultural heritage of Southern Thailand. From intricate bamboo weaving to traditional textile work, every item tells a story of our ancestors' wisdom and skill.
